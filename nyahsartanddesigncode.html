SCRATCH AND OTHER PROJECTS

In one game I made, which is called "PIG", it is where one of the two players must hit 100 total and win the game by the roll of the die. Each time they roll, the score will add up unless they roll a one, in which their turn total will reduce to zero and the other player will have a chance to roll. If you click "hold", your turn total will be added to your total score amount.
